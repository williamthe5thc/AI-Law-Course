Learning Activity 2: Assessing no-code chatbot platforms for legal applications

    Learning Activity 2: Video

Watch a 15-minute presentation exploring how legal professionals can implement chatbot technology without programming knowledge. Learn to evaluate no-code platforms based on essential legal features, compare pricing models, calculate ROI, and follow a systematic selection process. Discover best practices for content development, testing, and maintenance to ensure your chatbot implementation meets professional standards while enhancing client service and firm efficiency.

Key Concepts

  • No-Code Platform Selection: Identifying platforms suitable for legal applications
  • Essential Legal Features: Security, compliance, customization, document handling
  • ROI Calculation: Measuring cost vs. benefits for legal chatbot implementation
  • Content Development: Creating effective legal content for chatbots
  • Testing and Maintenance: Ensuring accuracy and ongoing relevance

Tips for Success

  • Prioritize security features - Client confidentiality must be maintained at all times
  • Consider scalability - Evaluate how the platform will grow with your practice
  • Document your decision process - Create a record of your platform evaluation
  • Start small - Begin with a narrowly defined use case to test effectiveness
  • Plan for maintenance - Build time for regular updates into your implementation plan
