Learning Activity 1: Understanding Chatbot Fundamentals in Legal Contexts

    Learning Activity 1: Video 

Watch this 7-minute presentation exploring how chatbot technology is transforming legal practice. Learn about different types of legal chatbots, their technical components, how they process information, and their practical applications in both client-facing and attorney-facing scenarios. Discover evaluation criteria to help assess which chatbot solutions might address specific needs within your legal organization while understanding both the benefits and limitations of this emerging technology.

Key Concepts

  • Types of Legal Chatbots: Rule-based vs. AI-powered, client-facing vs. attorney-facing
  • Technical Components: Natural language processing, knowledge base, interface design
  • Information Processing: How chatbots interpret queries and formulate responses
  • Practical Applications: Client intake, document assembly, legal research assistance
  • Evaluation Criteria: Accuracy, scope, customization, integration, maintenance

Tips for Success

  • Consider real-world applications - Think about how chatbots could address specific pain points in legal practice
  • Evaluate critically - Look beyond marketing claims to understand actual capabilities
  • Think about scope - Consider carefully defined use cases rather than overly broad applications
  • Understand the limitations - Recognize what chatbots can and cannot do in legal contexts
  • Consider ethical implications - Always keep professional responsibility in mind when evaluating technology
