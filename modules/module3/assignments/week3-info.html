Week 3 Assignment: Legal Chatbot Ethical Analysis

    Assignment: Legal Chatbot Ethical Analysis 

In this assignment, you will analyze how general-purpose chatbots handle legal questions and evaluate the ethical and practical implications based on the concepts learned in this module. This hands-on exercise will help you apply the frameworks discussed in the lectures to real-world AI interactions and develop critical thinking about the implementation of chatbots in legal contexts.

Assignment Overview

You will test a freely available AI chatbot with different types of legal queries, document the interactions, and analyze them through the ethical and legal frameworks discussed in the module. This exercise will help you understand the practical implications of implementing chatbots in legal settings and develop recommendations for responsible deployment.

Learning Objectives

This assignment will help you:

  • Apply ethical frameworks from the lectures to evaluate AI responses to legal questions
  • Identify potential unauthorized practice of law (UPL) issues in chatbot interactions
  • Analyze how different prompt approaches affect the quality and ethics of AI responses
  • Develop best practices for implementing chatbot solutions in legal contexts

Instructions:

  1. Select a Chatbot: Choose a freely available AI chatbot such as ChatGPT, Claude, Bard, or another accessible option.
  2. Conduct Three Test Interactions: Test the chatbot with the following types of legal queries:
    • Information Query: A general legal information question (e.g., "What is the difference between a will and a trust?").
    • Situation-Specific Query: A hypothetical legal scenario (e.g., "If someone slips and falls in my store, what are my legal responsibilities?").
    • Document Analysis Query: A request to analyze a simple legal concept (e.g., "Can you explain what this non-compete clause means?").
  3. Document Each Interaction: For each query, record:
    • The exact prompt you used
    • The chatbot's complete response
    • Any disclaimers or limitations the chatbot mentioned
    • How the chatbot handled potentially giving legal advice vs. information
  4. Analyze Your Interactions: Using the frameworks from the module, analyze each interaction across the following dimensions:
    • Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL) Risk Assessment
    • Privacy and Data Security Considerations
    • Professional Responsibility Implications
    • Implementation Recommendations
  5. Prepare Your Report: Create a 1-2 page analysis report (see submission template for details)

Key Concepts to Address

  • UPL Boundaries: Where information becomes advice in AI interactions
  • Disclaimer Effectiveness: How chatbots communicate limitations
  • Ethical Responsibility: Professional obligations when using AI tools
  • Implementation Frameworks: Best practices for legal chatbot deployment
  • Risk Assessment: Identifying and mitigating legal and ethical risks

Tips for Success

  • Be specific in your queries - Design clear, focused legal questions that test the chatbot's capabilities
  • Document reactions fully - Capture the complete interaction, including any disclaimers or qualifications
  • Apply frameworks precisely - Directly reference concepts from the module lectures in your analysis
  • Consider the real-world context - Frame your recommendations for practical implementation
  • Be critical but constructive - Identify both strengths and limitations in the chatbot's approach
