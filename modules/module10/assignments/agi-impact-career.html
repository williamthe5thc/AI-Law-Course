Final Presentation Assignment

    Module 10: Future-Proofing Your Career - Industry Impacts of AGI/ASI 

Assignment Overview

Prepare and deliver a strategic presentation analyzing how Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Artificial Superintelligence (ASI) will transform your specific industry, with a focus on developing a personal adaptation strategy. This assignment builds on previous modules by shifting from current AI applications to long-term transformative impacts of advanced AI systems.

Assignment Format

Prepare a 5-minute presentation (8-10 slides) with accompanying speaker notes that will serve as your detailed analysis. Your presentation will be delivered to the class, followed by a 2-minute Q&A session.

Presentation Structure:

  1. Introduction (30 seconds)
    • Your industry context
    • Why AGI/ASI matters specifically to your field
  2. Industry Transformation Analysis (2 minutes)
    • Current functions most vulnerable to transformation
    • Capability thresholds and timeline projections
    • Transformation scenarios
  3. Strategic Adaptation Plan (2 minutes)
    • Your competitive advantages
    • Skill development priorities
    • Positioning strategies
  4. Implementation Roadmap (30 seconds)
    • Key actions and timeline
    • Critical milestones
  5. Key Takeaway (15 seconds)
    • Most valuable insight from your analysis

Industry Analysis Framework

Complete the following sections for your presentation:

1. Industry Baseline (1-2 slides)

  • Current State: Briefly describe your industry and current AI adoption level
  • Critical Functions: Identify 3-5 core functions in your industry that advanced AI could transform
  • Decision Systems: Analyze how complex decisions are currently made in your field
  • Knowledge Structures: Describe specialized knowledge frameworks in your industry

2. AGI/ASI Transformation Analysis (2-3 slides)

  • Capability Thresholds: Identify specific AI capability improvements that would trigger major changes
  • Transformation Scenarios: Develop 2-3 scenarios for how AGI/ASI could reshape your industry
  • Timeline Projections: Estimate short-term (5 years), medium-term (10 years), and long-term (20+ years) impacts
  • Value Chain Disruption: Analyze which industry segments face earliest/deepest transformation

3. Strategic Adaptation Plan (2-3 slides)

  • Enduring Human Advantages: Identify 3-5 capabilities likely to remain valuable amid AGI/ASI adoption
  • Strategic Skill Development: Outline your personal skill development roadmap
  • New Roles and Opportunities: Identify emerging roles that will be created by AGI/ASI integration
  • Professional Positioning: Develop strategies to position yourself advantageously for these changes

4. Implementation Roadmap (1-2 slides)

  • 12-Month Action Plan: Outline concrete actions for the next year
  • Monitoring Framework: Identify key indicators and developments to track
  • Resource Requirements: Specify learning resources, connections, and tools needed
  • Milestone Markers: Define success indicators for your adaptation strategy

Research Guidelines

Your analysis should be informed by:

  • Current research on AGI/ASI development trajectories
  • Industry-specific forecasts and analysis
  • Expert perspectives on capability development timelines
  • Real-world examples of emerging advanced AI applications

Additional Resources

  • AGI Development Timeline Projections (2025) - Overview of expert consensus on development trajectories
  • Industry Transformation Case Studies - Examples of early AGI applications across sectors
  • Career Adaptation Frameworks - Strategic approaches to professional development
  • AI Capability Threshold Analysis - Identifying transformative capability inflection points

Evaluation Criteria

Criteria Description Points
Analytical Depth Thoughtful, well-researched analysis of AGI/ASI impacts 20
Industry Specificity Clear focus on your particular industry context 20
Strategic Thinking Practical, forward-looking adaptation strategies 20
Implementation Planning Concrete, actionable steps for career development 20
Presentation Quality Clear communication of complex ideas, effective use of visual aids 20

Submission Requirements

  • Submit your presentation slides (PPT, PDF, or Google Slides link)
  • Include detailed speaker notes with each slide
  • Provide a separate reference list with at least 5 quality sources
  • Be prepared to present during the assigned class session

Important Note:

Remember, this assignment is not about predicting the future with certainty, but developing a strategic, adaptable approach to professional development in an era of transformative AI capabilities. The goal is to demonstrate your ability to apply what you've learned about AGI/ASI to your specific professional context.

Key Concepts

  • AGI Impacts: Understanding the transformative potential of artificial general intelligence
  • Industry Analysis: Identifying sector-specific vulnerabilities and opportunities
  • Strategic Planning: Developing proactive career adaptation strategies
  • Implementation Roadmap: Creating concrete plans with measurable milestones
  • Professional Positioning: Identifying enduring human advantages in an AI-enhanced workplace

Tips for Success

  • Be specific - Focus your analysis on your particular professional context
  • Balance perspective - Consider both opportunities and challenges
  • Think strategically - Develop an actionable plan, not just theoretical considerations
  • Incorporate course concepts - Draw connections to what you've learned throughout the course
  • Use visual aids effectively - Create clear, engaging slides that enhance your presentation
