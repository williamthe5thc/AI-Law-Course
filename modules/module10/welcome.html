Module 10: How will AGI Impact YOUR Career?

Welcome to our final module! In this forward-looking exploration, we'll examine the potential impact of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) on your specific career path and professional development. As we conclude this course, you'll synthesize what you've learned to create a personalized AGI readiness plan and long-term professional development strategy.

OBJECTIVES

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Distinguish between current AI capabilities and potential AGI developments
  • Analyze how AGI might transform key aspects of your professional field
  • Identify which professional skills are likely to remain valuable in an AGI-enhanced workplace
  • Evaluate personal and organizational readiness for increasing AI integration
  • Develop a strategic plan for skill development in preparation for AGI advancements
  • Articulate ethical considerations specific to AGI in your professional context
  • Create a comprehensive portfolio demonstrating your AI competency and readiness

ACTIVITIES TO ACHIEVE THESE OBJECTIVES

In this culminating module, you'll complete the following activities:

  1. Learning Resources
    • Overview video: Exploring AI Tools in Professional Settings
  2. Quiz
    • Low-Stakes AGI Quiz
  3. Assignments
    • Module 10 Assignment: AGI impact on your career
    • Final Portfolio

Tips for Success

  • Be specific to your field - Focus your analysis on your particular professional context
  • Look beyond automation - Consider how AGI might enhance, not just replace, human capabilities
  • Integrate course concepts - Draw on all the modules you've completed to develop a comprehensive perspective
  • Balance optimism and caution - Consider both opportunities and challenges
  • Think strategically - Develop an actionable plan, not just theoretical considerations
  • Consider ethical dimensions - Include ethical reflections in your professional development strategy
  • Showcase your growth - Your final portfolio should demonstrate how far you've come in understanding AI
