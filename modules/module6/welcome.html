Module 6: Advanced Prompting Techniques

Welcome to Module 6, where you'll learn advanced prompting strategies that will significantly enhance your AI interactions. This module explores sophisticated techniques including Role-Based prompting, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Chain-of-Thought reasoning. By mastering these methods, you'll be able to generate more accurate, relevant, and useful outputs from AI systems in your professional work.

OBJECTIVES

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Define advanced prompting strategies
  • Identify when to use advanced prompting techniques
  • Create advanced prompts for specific tasks
  • Apply Role-Based prompting to simulate expert perspectives
  • Implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for document analysis
  • Utilize Chain-of-Thought reasoning for complex problem-solving
  • Recognize common pitfalls and implement guardrails in AI usage
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of different prompting approaches

ACTIVITIES TO ACHIEVE THESE OBJECTIVES

This module consists of two main learning activities that will build your understanding of advanced prompting techniques:

  1. Assignments
    • Module 6.1 Advanced Prompting Techniques
    • Module 6.2: Uses, Pitfalls, and Guardrails
  2. Practice Activities
    • Low-Stakes Quiz Advanced Prompting
  3. Discussion
    • Module 6 Discussion

Tips for Success

  • Practice actively - These techniques improve with hands-on experience
  • Apply to real scenarios - Try adapting these methods to your professional tasks
  • Be specific and clear - Detailed prompts yield better results
  • Test and iterate - Refine your prompts based on the responses you receive
  • Implement guardrails - Always verify AI outputs and be mindful of limitations
  • Match techniques to tasks - Different prompting approaches work best for specific purposes
