Assignment 2: AI Legal Research Integration

   Module 5 - Assignment 2: AI Legal Research Integration

In this assignment, you'll document how AI tools can enhance your legal research workflow by creating a presentation using the provided template.

Assignment Overview:

You will create a presentation that demonstrates how AI tools can be integrated into your legal research processes. This assignment builds on your tool analysis from Assignment 1, focusing now on practical implementation in your workflow.

Click Here to Access the Template

Assignment Requirements

Slide Section Requirements
Introduction Include your name and organization (if applicable)
Current Job Role & Legal Tasks
  • Describe your current legal role in 2-3 sentences
  • List and briefly explain 3 specific legal research tasks you regularly perform
  • Tasks should be specific enough to analyze (e.g., "Researching case precedents for motion practice" rather than just "Legal research")
Current Research Process
  • Create a step-by-step breakdown of your current workflow
  • Estimate time required for each step and total process
  • List specific resources you currently use
  • Note key challenges or inefficiencies
AI Tool Selection
  • Identify which AI tools best match your legal tasks
  • Select tools from those analyzed in Assignment 1
  • Provide clear reasoning for each tool selection
AI-Enhanced Workflow
  • Create a clear step-by-step workflow incorporating the AI tool
  • Highlight where and how the AI tool integrates with your process
  • Note any settings or configurations needed
Efficiency Metrics
  • Define 4-6 specific metrics to evaluate AI effectiveness
  • Include both quantitative and qualitative measures
  • Explain how you'll collect/measure each metric
Practical Experience
  • Select one task for hands-on comparison
  • Conduct the same research using both traditional and AI methods
  • Record specific processes, sources, and outputs

SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Make a copy of the Google Slides template (using the button above)
  2. Complete all sections following the guidelines on each slide
  3. Download your completed presentation as a PDF
  4. Submit the PDF through Canvas by clicking the "Submit Assignment" button

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Thoroughness and specificity of workflow documentation
  • Quality of AI tool selection justification
  • Practicality of integration strategy
  • Relevance and measurability of efficiency metrics
  • Depth of practical comparison analysis

Key Concepts

  • Workflow Integration: Strategic incorporation of AI tools into existing legal processes
  • Efficiency Analysis: Measuring the impact of AI on legal research productivity
  • Tool Selection Criteria: Matching specific AI tools to particular legal tasks
  • Comparative Methodology: Analyzing traditional vs. AI-enhanced approaches
  • Performance Metrics: Establishing meaningful measurement frameworks

Tips for Success

  • Be specific about your workflow - Include detailed steps in your current process
  • Focus on measurable improvements - Quantify time savings where possible
  • Document real-world testing - Include specific examples from your hands-on comparison
  • Consider implementation challenges - Address potential barriers to adoption
  • Use visuals effectively - Workflow diagrams help illustrate integration points
