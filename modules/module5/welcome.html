Module 5: AI Tools in Legal Settings

Welcome to Week 5! This module will explore the integration of AI tools in legal settings. You'll learn to identify, apply, assess, and manage AI tools for legal tasks while developing critical skills for detecting and correcting AI hallucinations.

OBJECTIVES

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Identify and compare AI tools for legal research
  • Apply AI tools to specific legal tasks in your professional context
  • Assess the correctness of AI output compared to traditional sources
  • Articulate strategies for combining AI tools with traditional research methods
  • Identify and manage AI hallucinations in legal research tools
  • Develop a critical thinking framework for the verification of AI-generated content

ACTIVITIES TO ACHIEVE THESE OBJECTIVES

To achieve these objectives, complete the following activities:

  1. Learning Activities
    • Learning Activity 1: Exploring AI Tools in Legal Settings
  2. Assignments
    • Assignment 1: AI Legal Tools Comparison
    • Assignment 2: AI Application for Legal Tasks and Output Assessment
  3. Practice Activities
    • Learning Activity 2: Interactive Quiz
  4. Discussion
    • Module 5 Discussion

Tips for Success

  • Verify all AI outputs - Always cross-check AI-generated legal information with authoritative sources
  • Document your process - Keep track of how you used AI tools to ensure reproducibility
  • Be specific with prompts - Clear instructions yield more accurate legal information
  • Understand tool limitations - Know what each legal AI tool is designed to do and not do
  • Combine methods - Use AI to augment, not replace, traditional legal research techniques
  • Apply critical thinking - Evaluate all information with professional legal judgment
