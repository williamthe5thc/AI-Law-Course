Key Points to Consider

  • How has AI evolved from its conceptual origins to today's powerful systems?
  • What were the major breakthroughs that accelerated AI development?

Tips for Viewing

  • Take notes on the timeline - Map out key developments by decade
  • Notice interdisciplinary connections - How AI development has been influenced by other fields
  • Reflect on acceleration - Consider how the pace of development has changed
  • Connect to legal applications - Think about when and how AI began intersecting with legal practice
