Module 1: Assignments

Assignment: AI History Impact Analysis

Due Date: [Your Due Date]

Points: 50

Instructions:

For this assignment, you will analyze how the historical development of AI has influenced current applications in the legal field.

Requirements:

  1. Historical Connection: Select one significant AI development or breakthrough from history (pre-2015) and explain how it laid the groundwork for current AI applications in law.
  2. Current Application Analysis: Identify a specific current AI application in the legal field that builds on this historical development.
  3. Impact Assessment: Analyze how this technology is changing legal practice, including both benefits and challenges.
  4. Future Projection: Based on historical patterns, project how this technology might evolve in the next 5-10 years and what implications this could have for legal professionals.
  5. Evidence and Sources: Support your analysis with at least 3 credible sources, properly cited.

Format:

  • 1000-1500 words (approximately 4-6 pages, double-spaced)
  • APA or Bluebook citation format
  • Include introduction and conclusion
  • Use headings to organize your paper according to the requirements above

Grading Criteria:

  • Historical Accuracy (20%): Accurate representation of the historical AI development
  • Connection to Present (25%): Clear and logical connection between historical development and current application
  • Impact Analysis (25%): Thoughtful analysis of benefits and challenges
  • Future Projection (20%): Plausible projection based on historical patterns
  • Writing Quality and Sources (10%): Clear writing, proper citations, and credible sources

Submission Guidelines:

Submit your paper as a Word document or PDF through the assignment portal.
