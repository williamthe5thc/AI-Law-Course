Module 1 Welcome Page

The Evolution of AI - Foundations and Fundamentals

Welcome to Module 1, where we'll explore the historical development of artificial intelligence (AI) and establish fundamental concepts that every working professional should understand. This module combines a historical overview of AI's evolution with an introduction to core AI concepts, providing you with essential context for effectively using AI tools in your professional practice.

OBJECTIVES

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Identify the major historical periods in AI development and their significance
  • Recognize key breakthroughs that advanced the field of artificial intelligence
  • Understand the pattern of "AI winters" and subsequent revivals throughout history
  • Explain how artificial intelligence differs from traditional software approaches
  • Describe the basic approaches of machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing
  • Understand what large language models are and how they fundamentally work
  • Recognize the current capabilities and limitations of modern AI systems
  • Identify ethical considerations for professionals using AI tools

Tips for Success

  • Connect history to the present. - Look for patterns in AI development that help explain current capabilities.
  • Focus on understanding the core concepts. - Don't worry about technical details at this stage.
  • Consider your professional context. - As you learn, think about how these concepts apply to your field.
  • Engage with the timeline. - The interactive timeline provides helpful visual context for AI's development.
  • Participate actively in discussions. - Sharing perspectives with peers enhances understanding.
  • Take notes on terminology. - Creating a personal AI glossary will help throughout the course.
  • Reflect on ethical implications. - Consider how AI's development raises important ethical questions.
