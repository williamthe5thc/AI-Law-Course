Assignment 3: AI Media Portfolio

    Module 4 - Assignment 3: Media Portfolio 

For this final assignment in Module 4, you will compile your work from Assignments 1 and 2 into a comprehensive digital portfolio that demonstrates your understanding of AI media creation tools and prompt engineering techniques. Your portfolio will showcase your ability to analyze different tools, document your prompt development process, and reflect on effective strategies for media creation.

Click Here to Access Portfolio Template

Portfolio Structure

1. Introduction (10 points)

Begin your portfolio with a brief introduction that explains:

  • Your overall approach to exploring AI media creation tools
  • The specific tools you selected and why
  • Your general process for developing and refining prompts
  • How these tools might be applicable in legal or professional contexts

2. Tool Comparison Summary (15 points)

Include your completed tool comparison matrix from Assignment 1, along with:

  • A summary of key findings from your tool exploration
  • Comparative analysis of strengths and limitations across platforms
  • Recommendations for specific use cases based on your analysis

3. Media Creation Documentation (30 points)

Incorporate your work from Assignment 2, including:

  • All three image creation examples with progressive prompt iterations
  • Video creation process documentation and final output link
  • Audio creation process documentation and final output link
  • Your analysis of effective prompting strategies for each media type

4. Reflection and Professional Applications (20 points)

Conclude your portfolio with a thoughtful reflection addressing:

  • Key learnings from your media creation experience
  • Patterns observed across different media types and platforms
  • Potential applications in your professional context or legal practice
  • Ethical considerations and best practices for responsible AI media creation
  • Areas for further exploration or improvement

Key Concepts to Address

  • Tool Evaluation Framework: Systematic approach to comparing AI media tools
  • Prompt Engineering Strategy: Principles for crafting effective prompts across media types
  • Process Documentation: Structured recording of the creative process and iterations
  • Media-Specific Parameters: Unique considerations for different media formats
  • Professional Application: Real-world implementation in legal or professional contexts

Tips for Success

  • Be comprehensive - Include all required components from previous assignments
  • Showcase your process - Document your thinking at each stage
  • Analyze patterns - Identify common principles across different media types
  • Connect to professional practice - Relate your findings to legal applications
  • Be visually organized - Structure your portfolio for clarity and readability

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: Single document (PDF or Google Doc link)
  • Media: Include screenshots of images, links to video/audio
  • Length: Comprehensive but focused (quality over quantity)
  • Due date: End of Module 4 (see course schedule)
  • Submission: Upload through the assignment portal
