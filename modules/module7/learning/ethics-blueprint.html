AI Ethics Blueprint: Foundations for Your Professional Portfolio

Part 1: Prompting Strategy Design (1 hour)

Design a comprehensive strategy for exploring AI ethics in your industry using three advanced techniques:

1.1 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

  • Curate 3-5 high-quality, industry-relevant sources on AI ethics
  • Create prompts that effectively incorporate this external knowledge
  • Develop a template for RAG prompts tailored to your industry

1.2 Chain of Thought (CoT) Reasoning

  • Design prompts that guide LLMs through step-by-step ethical reasoning
  • Break down complex ethical questions into logical components
  • Create a CoT template for analyzing professional ethical dilemmas

1.3 Role-Based Prompting

  • Develop prompts that assign specific perspectives to the LLM
  • Create role configurations representing diverse stakeholders
  • Build a framework for synthesizing multi-perspective insights

DELIVERABLE

Submit a 1-2 page prompting strategy document with templates and industry-specific examples for each technique.

Pro Tip: When creating your RAG strategy, select sources that represent different perspectives on AI ethics in your industry, such as technical documents, policy frameworks, and case studies.

Part 2: Ethical Area Exploration (2.5 hours)

Select two of the following focus areas to explore using your prompting techniques:

Ethical Frameworks and Implementation

  • Human oversight requirements for AI systems
  • Operational values and escalation paths
  • Embedding ethics into AI workflows

Safety and Risk Management

  • NIST AI Risk Management Framework implementation
  • Preventing data poisoning and adversarial attacks
  • Incident response frameworks for AI

Regulatory Landscape

  • EU AI Act implications for your industry
  • Industry-specific compliance requirements
  • Transparency and accountability mechanisms

Environmental and Social Impact

  • AI carbon footprint in your industry
  • Social impact of AI adoption
  • Measuring societal impact metrics

For each selected area:

  1. Apply each prompting technique to explore implications
  2. Document your prompts, responses, and iterative improvements
  3. Compare the effectiveness of different techniques
  4. Create a comprehensive summary, plan, or analysis

Industry Context Examples

Healthcare: Focus on diagnostic algorithms, care automation, patient data management

Finance: Examine loan decisions, fraud detection, algorithmic discrimination prevention

Manufacturing: Address workforce automation, safety monitoring, environmental impact

Retail: Investigate customer privacy, personalization ethics, recommendation systems

DELIVERABLE

A 500-750 word document for each of your two selected ethical areas (1000-1500 words total).

Pro Tip: Document both successful and unsuccessful prompting approaches. The evolution of your prompts provides valuable insights into the strengths and limitations of different techniques.

  • How your prompting strategies evolved throughout the assignment

DELIVERABLE

Completed evaluation template and 500-750 word reflection.

Pro Tip: In your reflection, consider the meta-ethical implications of using AI to explore AI ethics. Does the medium influence the message? What biases might be reinforced or challenged?

Key Concepts

  • AI Ethics Integration: Applying ethical frameworks to real-world AI implementations
  • Multi-perspective Analysis: Examining ethical issues from diverse stakeholder viewpoints
  • Advanced Prompting: Using sophisticated techniques to elicit nuanced ethical reasoning
  • Comparative Evaluation: Assessing which prompting approaches work best for ethical exploration
  • Industry Application: Connecting ethical principles to specific professional contexts
