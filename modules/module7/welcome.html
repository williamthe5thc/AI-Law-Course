Module 7: AI Ethics & Advanced Prompting Techniques

Welcome to this two-week module on AI Ethics and Advanced Prompting Techniques. You'll explore ethical dimensions of AI implementation in professional settings while developing sophisticated prompting skills with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Chain of Thought (CoT) reasoning, and role-based prompting approaches. This comprehensive exploration will enhance both your technical capabilities and ethical awareness in professional AI applications.

OBJECTIVES

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Develop proficiency with RAG, Chain of Thought, and role-based prompting techniques
  • Critically analyze ethical challenges in professional AI implementation
  • Create effective prompting strategies to explore complex ethical topics
  • Evaluate the effectiveness of different prompting approaches
  • Reflect on the ethical implications of using LLMs for decision-making

ACTIVITIES TO ACHIEVE THESE OBJECTIVES

Tips for Success

  • Start early - This assignment requires thoughtful exploration of complex ethical issues
  • Select diverse ethical areas - Choose topics that interest you and represent different challenges
  • Iteratively improve your prompts - Your first attempts will likely need refinement
  • Document your process - Keep detailed notes on what works and what doesn't
  • Use high-quality sources for RAG - Academic papers and industry reports will yield better results
  • Try multiple perspectives - Role-based prompting is most effective when examining diverse viewpoints
  • Reflect throughout - Consider ethical implications as you proceed, not just at the end
