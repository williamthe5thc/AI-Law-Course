Module 2 Discussion

    Discussion: Implementing Prompt Engineering in Legal Work 

Overview:

In this discussion, you will identify a routine task from your professional work that could benefit from AI assistance through prompt engineering. You will demonstrate your understanding of different prompting techniques by creating specific examples for your chosen task.

Key Takeaway: Understanding how AI prompting can be applied across diverse professional settings helps you identify transferable strategies and best practices for implementing these skills in your own work environment.

Instructions to prepare for your discussion post:

1. Identify and describe a recurring work task that could be enhanced through prompt engineering.

2. For your identified task, create one example for each prompt type learned this week (Zero-shot, and Few-Shot).

3. Brainstorm how to effectively apply these prompt strategies.

Discussion Instructions:

Step 1: After completing the preparation instructions above, respond to the following questions by hitting "Reply" at the bottom of this page.

Your post should:

Part 1: Task Identification (150-200 words)

Identify and describe a recurring task from your professional work that could be enhanced through prompt engineering. Your description should include:

  • The context and purpose of the task
  • Current challenges or inefficiencies in completing this task
  • Why this task would benefit from AI assistance
  • The desired outcome when using AI for this task

Part 2: Prompt Engineering Examples

For your identified task, create one example for each prompt type learned this week (Zero-shot, and Few-Shot).

  1. Zero-Shot Prompt
    • Direct instruction without examples
    • Explain why this level of instruction would or wouldn't be sufficient
  2. Few-Shot Prompt
    • 2-3 examples followed by the target task
    • Explain how multiple examples improve the AI's understanding

Part 3: Reflection (100-150 words)

Conclude with a brief reflection on:

  • Which prompting technique you believe would be most effective for your task and why
  • How implementing this approach could improve your workflow
  • Any potential limitations or challenges you foresee

Step 2: Respond to two peer posts. If a post already has three responses, consider finding another one in which to respond.

To foster thoughtful responses in discussion boards:

  1. Be Respectful: Acknowledge and build on peers' points.
  2. Use Evidence: Support your comments with examples or evidence.
  3. Ask Questions: Pose open-ended questions to deepen the conversation.
  4. Reflect: Connect others' ideas to your own thoughts.
  5. Stay Focused: Keep responses relevant and on-topic.
  6. Be Clear: Write concisely and clearly.
  7. Analyze: Provide insightful analysis or alternative perspectives.
  8. Value Diversity: Encourage and respect different viewpoints.

Key Concepts

  • Practical Application: Identifying relevant scenarios for prompting techniques
  • Zero-Shot Implementation: Creating direct prompts without examples
  • Few-Shot Strategies: Using examples to guide AI responses
  • Task Analysis: Evaluating which prompt type is most suitable for specific tasks
  • Workflow Integration: Determining how to incorporate prompting into legal practice

Tips for Success

  • Be specific - Choose a task you regularly perform rather than a hypothetical scenario
  • Consider ethical implications - Address any potential ethical considerations in your task
  • Show your work - Include complete prompt examples that others could use as models
  • Focus on outcomes - Explain how the prompting approach improves the final result
  • Be realistic - Acknowledge limitations and challenges in your reflection
