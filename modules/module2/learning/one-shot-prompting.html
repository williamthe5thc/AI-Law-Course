One-Shot and Few-Shot Prompting

  Learning Activity 3 Overview:

While large-language models demonstrate remarkable zero-shot capabilities, they seem to struggle with more complex tasks. Few-shot prompting can be used as a technique to enable in-context learning where we provide examples, files, or definitions in the prompt.

Key Takeaways

  • Example-based learning - Few-shot prompting leverages examples to guide AI responses
  • Improved accuracy - Provides more consistent results for complex or specialized tasks
  • Format control - Helps maintain specific output structures across different queries
  • Domain adaptation - Enables better performance in specialized professional contexts
  • Progressive approach - Start with zero-shot, advance to one-shot or few-shot as needed
