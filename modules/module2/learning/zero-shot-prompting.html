  Learning Activity 2.2 (Self-Check):

As you read through these Legal Examples, see if you can identify the 4 pillars of prompting in each 'Quality Prompt':

Example 1: Contract Analysis

Bad Prompt ❌: "Check this contract."

Quality Prompt ✅: "Analyze this employment contract for potential liability issues, focusing specifically on non-compete clauses, termination conditions, and compensation terms. Highlight any ambiguous language that could lead to legal disputes."

Example 2: Case Research

Bad Prompt ❌: "Find similar cases." 

Quality Prompt ✅: "Identify relevant precedents for a case involving workplace discrimination based on remote work accommodation requests during COVID-19. Focus on ADA compliance and reasonable accommodation standards."

Key Points to Remember

  • Specificity is crucial - Vague prompts lead to vague responses
  • Include context - Help the AI understand the situation and requirements
  • Define focus areas - Direct attention to the most important aspects
  • Set quality standards - Indicate what makes a good response for your purposes
