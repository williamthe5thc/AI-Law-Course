One-Shot and Few-Shot Prompting

LEARNING ACTIVITY 3 OVERVIEW:

While large-language models demonstrate remarkable zero-shot capabilities, they seem to struggle with more complex tasks. Few-shot prompting can be used as a technique to enable in-context learning where we provide examples, files, or definitions in the prompt.

Prompting Guide - Few-Shot Prompting

Key Points to Remember About Few-Shot Prompting

  • Examples matter - The quality and relevance of your examples significantly impact results
  • Pattern recognition - AI learns the pattern from your examples and applies it to new inputs
  • Context window limits - Be mindful of the maximum context length when providing multiple examples
  • Diversity helps - Include examples that cover different aspects of your target task
  • Legal application - Particularly useful for specialized legal formats and domain-specific tasks
