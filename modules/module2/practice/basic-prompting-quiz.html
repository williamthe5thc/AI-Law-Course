Low-Stakes Basic Prompting Quiz

    Knowledge Check: Basic Prompting 

This low-stakes quiz will help you assess your understanding of basic prompting concepts, including prompt elements, zero-shot prompting, and few-shot prompting techniques. You can take the quiz multiple times if needed.

Points: 10 points

Questions: 10 multiple-choice questions

Time Limit: None

Sample Questions:

1. Which of the following is NOT one of the four key elements of an effective prompt?

A) Task instruction

B) Context information

C) Technical jargon

D) Format requirements

2. When is zero-shot prompting most appropriate?

A) When working with highly specialized legal terminology

B) When the task is straightforward and requires basic capabilities

C) When the output format is complex and specific

D) When creating a customized document template

Note: In the actual course, you would see all 10 questions and be able to submit your answers.

Key Concepts Being Tested

  • Prompt Components: Understanding the essential elements of effective prompts
  • Zero-Shot Prompting: Recognizing when and how to use this approach
  • One-Shot and Few-Shot Prompting: Understanding example-based prompting
  • Prompt Engineering Principles: Applying best practices for different scenarios
  • Legal Applications: Recognizing appropriate prompting techniques for legal contexts
