Module 9: AI Learning Tools for Enhancement

Welcome to this interactive module on AI tools for learning enhancement! Over the next 3 hours, you'll explore how AI can transform your learning experience, test out tools that interest you, and share insights with your peers.

OBJECTIVES

This module will guide you through exploring how AI tools can enhance your learning and professional development.

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Identify at least three ways AI can enhance your learning or professional practice
  • Explore and test at least one AI learning tool relevant to your needs
  • Compare the efficiency of traditional methods versus AI-assisted approaches
  • Share insights and experiences with AI learning tools with peers
  • Evaluate the potential impact of AI tools on your future learning strategies
  • Recognize ethical considerations when implementing AI in educational contexts

ACTIVITIES TO ACHIEVE THESE OBJECTIVES

Complete the following activities to build your understanding of AI learning tools and their practical applications:

  1. Introduction to AI for Learning Enhancement (30 minutes)
    • Watch the instructional video (12 minutes)
    • Review the AI Learning Tools List (18 minutes)
  2. Guided AI Tool Exploration (90 minutes)
    • Select a learning challenge to address (10 minutes)
    • Choose an AI tool to explore (10 minutes)
    • Document your traditional approach (baseline) (15 minutes)
    • Test the AI tool (40 minutes)
    • Compare results and reflect (15 minutes)
  3. Sharing & Discussion (60 minutes)
    • Complete your exploration template (30 minutes)
    • Share your experiences in the discussion board (30 minutes)

Tips for Success

  • Conduct a personal inventory - Assess your current learning challenges before selecting a tool
  • Start small with AI tools - Begin with one specific task rather than trying to transform everything at once
  • Practice effective prompting - Spend time crafting clear instructions when using AI tools
  • Document your experiences - Keep track of both successes and challenges as you test AI tools
  • Focus on augmentation, not replacement - Look for ways AI can enhance your existing learning strategies
  • Share insights with peers - Discussing AI tools with colleagues multiplies learning opportunities
  • Be curious but critical - Approach AI tools with an open mind but evaluate their effectiveness objectively
