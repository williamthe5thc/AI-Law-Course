Assignment 1: AI Tool Exploration Templates

    Assignment: AI Tool Exploration 

Assignment Overview

In this assignment, you will select and explore an AI learning tool that addresses a specific challenge in your learning or professional practice. You will document your experience using structured templates to compare traditional approaches with AI-assisted methods.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify and articulate specific learning or professional challenges that might benefit from AI assistance
  • Experience and evaluate AI tools in a structured, reflective manner
  • Compare traditional and AI-assisted approaches to specific tasks
  • Articulate the potential impact of AI tools on learning and professional practice

Timeline

Total estimated time: 90 minutes

  • Tool selection: 20 minutes
  • Document traditional approach: 15 minutes
  • Test AI tool: 40 minutes
  • Reflection and comparison: 15 minutes

Exploration Templates

Use the following templates to guide your exploration of an AI learning tool:

1. Learning Challenge Identification

Describe a specific learning or professional challenge you'd like to address:

  • What specific task or process do you currently find challenging?
  • Why is this challenge important to your learning or professional practice?
  • How do you currently approach this challenge?

Example: "I struggle with synthesizing information from multiple legal cases quickly. This skill is critical for brief writing and case analysis in my practice. Currently, I read each case fully and take manual notes, which is time-consuming."

2. AI Tool Selection

Identify an AI tool that might help with your challenge:

  • Which tool did you select and why?
  • What features of this tool seem most relevant to your challenge?
  • Have you used similar tools before? If so, how was your experience?

Example: "I selected Casetext because it offers AI-powered case summarization features. The ability to extract key points automatically seems relevant to my challenge. I've used basic legal research tools but none with AI summarization capabilities."

3. Baseline Documentation

Document your traditional approach:

  • Describe your current step-by-step process
  • Note approximately how long each step takes
  • Identify pain points or inefficiencies in your current approach
  • If possible, save examples of your work using the traditional method

Example: "My current process involves: 1) Reading the full case (30-45 min per case), 2) Taking detailed notes (15-20 min), 3) Creating a summary (10-15 min)..."

4. AI Tool Testing

Document your experience using the AI tool:

  • How did you set up or access the tool?
  • What prompts or inputs did you provide?
  • What results did you receive?
  • What adjustments did you make to improve results?
  • Save examples of your work using the AI tool

Example: "I created a Casetext account and uploaded the case. I used the 'Key Points' feature, then tried the 'Custom Query' option with the prompt 'summarize the court's reasoning on X issue'..."

5. Comparison and Reflection

Compare the traditional and AI-assisted approaches:

  • Time comparison: Which method was faster?
  • Quality comparison: Which method produced better results?
  • Efficiency comparison: Which method required less effort?
  • Learning comparison: Which method helped you understand the material better?
  • What surprised you about the AI tool?
  • What limitations did you encounter?
  • How might you integrate this tool into your learning or professional practice?

Example: "The AI-assisted approach was significantly faster (15 min vs. 60 min per case). The quality was acceptable for initial review but missed some nuanced reasoning. I was surprised by how accurate the tool was in identifying key holdings..."

Key Concepts

  • Structured Exploration: Systematic approach to evaluating AI tools
  • Baseline Comparison: Measuring AI performance against traditional methods
  • Critical Evaluation: Assessing benefits and limitations of AI tools
  • Process Documentation: Recording both methods for comprehensive analysis
  • Implementation Planning: Strategies for integrating tools into workflows

Tips for Success

  • Be specific - Choose a focused task rather than a broad challenge
  • Document thoroughly - Take screenshots and notes throughout both processes
  • Try multiple approaches - Experiment with different prompts and settings
  • Be honest about limitations - Critically assess both benefits and drawbacks
  • Consider long-term implications - Reflect on how these tools might change your practices
