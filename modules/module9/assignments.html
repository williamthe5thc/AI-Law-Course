Module 9: Assignments

Assignment 1: AI Tool Exploration Report

Due Date: [Your Due Date]

Points: 50

Instructions:

Complete your exploration of at least one AI learning tool and prepare a report documenting:

  • The learning challenge you selected
  • The AI tool you explored
  • Your traditional approach (baseline)
  • Your experience using the AI tool
  • A comparison of results between traditional and AI-assisted methods
  • Reflections on the potential impact on your learning/professional strategies
  • Any ethical considerations you identified

Submission Guidelines:

Your report should be 2-3 pages, double-spaced, and include screenshots or examples as needed.
